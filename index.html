Five Nights at Freddy’s – Fan Theories

This is a fan-made website about Five Nights at Freddy’s. Here you can read theories, ideas, and opinions from the community.

Popular Theories

  • The Bite of '87: A theory suggests that the animatronic responsible for the Bite of '87 was actually Mangle, not Foxy.
  • Phone Guy's Identity: Some fans believe that Phone Guy is actually a former employee who survived an attack and is now guiding new night guards.
  • Multiple Timelines: There is a theory that each game represents a different timeline, explaining inconsistencies in the lore.
  • Golden Freddy's Origins: Some believe Golden Freddy is possessed by two children's spirits: Cassidy and the crying child from the fourth part of the franchise.
  • The Puppet's Role: A popular theory suggests that The Puppet is the key to understanding the entire FNAF lore, acting as a guardian for the children's souls.
  • Springtrap's Past: Fans theorize that Springtrap is not just William Afton but also contains remnants of the souls of the children he harmed.
  • The Missing Children Incident: Some theories propose that the incident involved more children than officially stated, leading to additional hauntings.
